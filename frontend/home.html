Backend computes, frontend animates

Your input goes to a Python Flask API. The algorithm runs for real, every step is captured as JSON, and the canvas animates it frame by frame. Benchmarks are persisted to MySQL so you can compare Bubble vs Merge, Quick vs Heap, or A* vs Dijkstra over time.
